The operating system behind Junction AI's agency model. Built on DBH data, proven in production.

The DBH Story

Deep Blue Health was our proving ground. Everything we know about autonomising marketing was learned here.

DBH started as a traditional DTC brand with disconnected channels, manual reporting, and no unified view. Over months we built: 27 cron tasks, 11 AI agents, real-time data pipelines, and weekly CEO reports that wrote themselves.

Single biggest insight: the key is knowing the data. Claude learns from data, extracts insights, understands what works. But data without a dashboard is noise — you need a system to make it speak.

What emerged is not just tools but a philosophy: integrate every platform, analyse every channel to first principles, build systems that run fundamentals while continuously testing what could work better. Then report ruthlessly.

"Reflection followed by strategy followed by execution followed by analysis and reflection again. This is the loop. AI analyses and sets briefs based on learnings and does the grunt execution work so much faster — which allows operating at an elite level."

📊
Data First, Always
Every decision starts with data. Integrate all platforms Day 1. Shopify+Klaviyo+Meta+Google together tell the full story — any one alone is misleading.
⚙️
Systems Over Heroics
Each channel runs fundamentals through automated systems. The cron runs. Reports generate. Flows fire. Heroic last-minute efforts mean the system failed.
🔄
The Improvement Loop
Reflection, then strategy, then execution, then analysis. Every sprint ends with: did our test meet the hypothesis? Feed learnings back. The system gets smarter every cycle.
🤝
Human + AI = Elite
AI analyses data, sets briefs, executes at speed. Humans own creative, client relationships, quality oversight. The combination operates at a level neither can alone.

What Should Have Been Done From Day 1

Mistakes that cost real money and real data. Now they're rules.

UTM Parameters on All Paid Traffic
What happened: Pure Pets March campaign launched without UTM params in ad destination URLs.
Impact: 69% of orders had MISSING_SOURCE. Meta reported 4 conversions; Shopify attributed 0.
The Rule: UTM params are a Day 1 gate. No paid campaign launches without them verified.
Direct Platform Access
What happened: Google Ads managed through agency intermediary. Tom couldn't access data directly.
Impact: Delayed reporting, inability to optimise, dependency on third party.
The Rule: Admin access to every platform before contract signed. Non-negotiable.
Attribution Windows Agreed Upfront
What happened: Different platforms use different attribution windows. Conflicting numbers confused reporting.
Impact: Client confusion, misaligned expectations on channel performance.
The Rule: Agree attribution methodology (7-day click, 1-day view) with client before first campaign.
Customer Intelligence DB from Day 1
What happened: Customer DB was retrofitted months into the engagement.
Impact: Lost months of acquisition channel data, LTV modelling was incomplete.
The Rule: customer_intelligence.db initialised on Day 1 of onboarding. Every order tagged from the start.
Brand vs Non-Brand Search Separated
What happened: Brand and non-brand Google Ads mixed in same campaigns.
Impact: Couldn't identify true non-brand performance. Branded search at capacity but budget kept increasing.
The Rule: Separate campaigns from Day 1. Brand search is branded demand, not acquisition.
Klaviyo Flows Before Acquisition
What happened: Meta ads running before welcome/post-purchase flows were properly set up.
Impact: Paid acquisition traffic hitting dead-end experience. Repeat purchase rate suppressed.
The Rule: All core Klaviyo flows live and tested before running any paid acquisition.

What Genuinely Works

Validated by real revenue. These are the foundations every client campaign builds on.

Video Creative at TOF
Data: 21% ATC rate (video) vs 7% (static). Emotion-led storytelling beats feature/benefit at cold traffic.
Why: People buy from people. Video builds the micro-trust needed for a first click.
Email List as Owned Asset
Data: Email consistently 40-60% of total revenue. Compounds regardless of Meta algorithm changes.
Why: The list is yours. No platform can take it away. The longer you build it, the more it's worth.
Fortnightly Sprint Structure
Data: Product-focused fortnights created measurable outcomes and clear before/after comparisons.
Why: Focus beats diffusion. One product across all channels for two weeks = compounding signal.
Weekly CEO Reports Build Trust
Data: Tony engaged most deeply with structured reports. Approval cycles got faster.
Why: When the data is clear and structured, trust builds. Clients advocate for you internally.
Health Science Content Drives Repeat Purchase
Data: Helix-style educational content correlated with higher LTV customers.
Why: Education creates conviction. Convicted customers don't churn. They refer.
Multi-Platform Cross-Reference is Non-Negotiable
Data: Meta reported 4 conversions on a campaign that Shopify attributed 0 to.
Why: Every platform lies a little. The truth is in the intersection of all of them.

Proven on DBH data. Apply to every DTC client campaign.

#1

Trust + Social Proof

7.78x Meta ROAS | 48.5% Email Open Rate

Lead with real customer testimonials and results. Customer voice > brand voice. Attracts quality customers with higher LTV. Use in EVERY campaign.

Evidence: GLM Social Proof + Trust campaign. 25 conversions from $309 spend.

USE IN EVERY CAMPAIGN
#2

Exclusivity + Scarcity

8.45x Meta ROAS | $9,259 Single Email Revenue

Limited time + genuine deadline. Loss aversion is 2x stronger than gain motivation. Max 4-6x per year to maintain credibility.

Evidence: Black Friday 8.45x ROAS. "Early Access" = $9,259 single email.

MAX 4-6x/YEAR
#3

Gift Psychology

77.1% Email Open Rate | 4.15% Click Rate

"Free X with purchase" beats "X% off" every time. Same economics, different psychology. Gift = reward. Discount = transaction.

Evidence: "Spend $150, get free moisturiser" — highest open rate in entire DBH dataset.

ALWAYS FRAME AS REWARD
