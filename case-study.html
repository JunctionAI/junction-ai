Case Study

Deep Blue Health:
From Minimal Marketing to Full-Service Growth Engine

How one operator and an AI-powered marketing system replaced an entire agency team — and delivered 100% revenue growth in three months.

Book a Discovery Call See the Numbers
The Challenge

A great product line with no marketing engine

Deep Blue Health is a DTC supplement brand in New Zealand with a loyal customer base and strong product line. But when it came to marketing, almost nothing was running.

⚠️

Fragmented Marketing

No unified strategy across channels. Each platform operated in isolation with no shared data, no shared learnings, and no coherent customer journey.

🗒

No Unified System

No central source of truth for performance data. Shopify, Klaviyo, Meta, and Google each told a different story. Attribution was guesswork.

✋

Manual Execution

Every campaign, every email, every ad had to be built by hand. Output was severely limited by available hours and headcount.

📈

Poor Attribution

No per-order tracking. No customer acquisition tagging. No way to know which channels were actually driving revenue versus claiming credit.

👥

Limited Headcount

A small team stretched across operations, product, and everything else. No dedicated marketing resource. No agency. No bandwidth.

🕑

Missed Opportunities

No email flows. No SEO. No retargeting. No replenishment triggers. Revenue was being left on the table across every channel, every day.

The System We Built

24 AI agents. 38 modules. One autonomous stack.

We deployed a full marketing intelligence system — purpose-built for Deep Blue Health. Every channel connected. Every insight shared. Every cycle smarter than the last.

🤖

Autonomous Campaign Management

Meta Ads managed with Shopify-verified ROAS tracking. Auto-pause underperformers. Auto-adjust budgets. A/B winner selection. Campaign drafting from intelligence — not guesswork.

🎯

Real Attribution

Per-order tracking matched to Shopify. Every customer tagged with acquisition channel and cohort. One source of truth — not inflated platform numbers. CPA:LTV cohort analysis monthly.

🔁

Replenishment Triggers

Product consumption rates calculated per SKU. Klaviyo events fired automatically when customers are due to reorder. Revenue recovered before it's lost.

📚

Daily SEO Publishing

Autonomous SEO agent publishes one article daily to the Shopify blog. DietarySupplement schema markup. Google Search Console feedback loop. AI citation monitoring for AEO.

🧠

Cross-Agent Intelligence

24 agents share knowledge through an event bus. What the ads agent learns, the email agent uses. What the SEO agent discovers, the strategy agent incorporates. Intelligence compounds.

📊

Automated Reporting

Weekly CEO reports generated automatically with real data. Daily briefings across all channels. Rule engine creates Asana tasks from business logic. Nothing falls through the cracks.
